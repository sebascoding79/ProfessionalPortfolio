How I work when tackling a new problem

Research Phase

Firstly, it's important to understand the problem, especially the business aspect so the customer always comes up on the winning end, so I like asking questions and figuring the outcomes to edge cases and common scenarios.

Design and Plan

So now that I understand the problem and what is the expected outcome, I like to focus on the software development point of view and answering questions like: Are we adding a new table? What new objects could we be making? Are we toucing current functionality. I am not afraid to ask for second opinions from architects or lead engineers to make the best choice and I learn something new along the way.

Execute

The coding challenge begins here and once that part is done, I love presenting what I have done to my peers and business analysts to confirm the outcome of the added code and put my pull request out for revision During that time I figure out if I need to reach out to Site Reliability Engineers if we are updating tables in the database or need to schedule an emergency deployment to staging or production.
